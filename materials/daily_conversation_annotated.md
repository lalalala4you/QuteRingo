## 会話の相槌 (Conversational Responses)

| 日本語 | 意味 | Usage |
|--------|------|-------|
| なるほど | I see | Understanding |
| そうですね | That's right / Let me think | Agreement / Thinking |
| 本当ですか | Really? | Surprise |
| すごい | Amazing! | Impression |
| やっぱり | As I thought | Confirmation |
| ちょっと… | Well... (hesitation) | Soft refusal |
| どうしようかな | What should I do... | Pondering |
